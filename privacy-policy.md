# Privacy Policy for Starshelf Alpha

*Last updated: July 13, 2026*

Starshelf Alpha ("the app") is a booklist app for browsing award-winning science fiction books. This policy explains, in plain language, what happens with your data.

## We don't collect personal data

Starshelf Alpha has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no way for us to identify you. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## What the app stores

- Book, author, and award information is public reference data, fetched from our database to display in the app.
- Your personal reading list — which books you've marked as "owned" or "read" — is saved only on your own device. It is never sent to us or anyone else.

## No tracking, analytics, or ads

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs of any kind.

## No data sharing

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is none to share, sell, or disclose to third parties.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, an updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
